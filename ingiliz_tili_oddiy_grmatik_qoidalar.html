Oddiy Grammatik Qoidalar

Ingliz tilining asosiy grammatik qoidalari — sodda va amaliy
Bo'limlar Zamonaviy (Tenses) Articles Savollar & Negatsiya

Bu sahifa ingliz tilining eng kerakli grammatik qoidalarini soddalashtirilgan shaklda beradi — parts of speech, tenses, articles, questions, negation, prepositions va oddiy mashqlar bilan.

Parts of speech (So'z turkumlari)
  • Noun — ism (person, place, thing): cat, city, book.
  • Pronoun — olmosh: I, you, he, she, it, we, they.
  • Verb — fe'l: go, eat, be, have.
  • Adjective — sifat: big, small, beautiful.
  • Adverb — ravish: quickly, very, often.
  • Preposition — qarama-qarshilik: in, on, at.
  • Conjunction — bog'lovchi: and, but, or.
  • Interjection — hayrat: Wow!, Oh!.
Maslahat: so'z turkumini bilish gap tuzishda yordam beradi — masalan, sifatlar (adjectives) odatda noun oldida keladi: big house.
Oddiy zamonlar (Simple Tenses)

Simple Present — hozirgi oddiy

Oddiy odatlar yoki umumiy haqiqatlar uchun.

I/You/We/They + verb
He/She/It + verb(s/es)

Examples:
I eat breakfast. / He eats breakfast.

Simple Past — o'tgan oddiy

O'tgan vaqt haqidagi harakatlar uchun.

Subject + verb (past form)

Examples:
She visited her friend yesterday. (visit -> visited)

Simple Future — kelasi oddiy

Kelajakdagi niyatlar yoki voqealar uchun.

Subject + will + base verb

Examples:
They will come tomorrow.
Maslahat: Boshlovchilar uchun avvalo ushbu uch zamonni mustahkamlash foydali — keyin boshqa continuous yoki perfect turlarga o'ting.
Articles (a, an, the)
ArticleQachon ishlatiladi
a / anUmumiy, hisobli, biror narsa haqida boshlang'ich ma'lumot: a dog, an apple (an — unli tovushdan oldin).
theAniq narsa — oldin aytilgan yoki kontekstda ma'lum bo'lsa: the book on the table.
zero articleUmumiy nomlar yoki noaniq ko'pchalik: Water is essential.
Savollar va inkorlar (Questions & Negation)

Oddiy savol

Do/Does + subject + verb?
Did + subject + verb (past)?
Will + subject + verb?

Examples: Do you like tea? Does she work here? Did they come? Will he call?

Negation

Don't / Doesn't (present)
Didn't (past)
Will not / won't (future)

I don't like coffee. He doesn't play football. They didn't come.
Eslatma: "doesn't" uchun verbning asosiy shakli (base) ishlatiladi: He doesn't go (not 'goes').
Prepositions (joy va vaqt)
PrepositionMisol
inin the room, in July
onon the table, on Monday
atat the bus stop, at 5 pm
Maslahat: vaqt uchun 'in' oylar va yillar, 'on' kunlar, 'at' aniq vaqtlar bilan ishlatiladi.
Subject-Verb Agreement

Subject va verb orasida kelishuv bo'lishi kerak — singular subject bilan singular verb (He/She/It qo'shimcha -s bilan), plural bilan basic form.

She walks. / They walk.
The dog barks. / The dogs bark.
Punctuation (Tinishing belgilar)
  • Period (.) — gap oxiri.
  • Question mark (?) — savol.
  • Comma (,) — ro'yxat yoki ichki qo'shimchalar.
  • Apostrophe (') — egallik: John's book, yoki qisqartmalar: I'm.
Mashqlar
  1. To'ldiring: I ____ (go) to school yesterday. (past tense)
  2. Tanlang: She (go/goes) to work every day.
  3. Inkor qiling: They play football. → __________
  4. Savol yozing: You are happy. → __________?
Ushbu mashqlarni ishlash orqali ingliz tilidagi bilimlaringizni mustaxkamlab boring!
Quick tips
  • Har kuni 10 ta oddiy gap yozing (present, past, future).
  • Oddiy zamonlarni o‘rganib chiqqach, continuous va perfectga o'ting.
  • O'qish va gapirishni birga mashq qiling — grammar amalda yaxshi o'rganiladi.
